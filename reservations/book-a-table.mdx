Whether you're planning a casual catch-up with friends or a special dinner with family, reserving a table at Boost Café ensures your group has a comfortable spot waiting when you arrive. We accept reservations for parties of 4 or more — if you're stopping by with a smaller group, you're always welcome to walk in and we'll do our best to seat you promptly.

## How to Book

You can reserve a table through the **Boost Café app**, our **website**, or by **calling the café directly**. Reservations can be made up to **30 days in advance** and no later than **2 hours before** your desired arrival time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App or Website">
    Launch the Boost Café app on your phone or visit our website and navigate to the **Reservations** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Location">
    Select the Boost Café location you'd like to visit from the list of available venues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a Date and Time">
    Browse available time slots for your chosen date. Slots shown in real time reflect current availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Party Size">
    Enter the number of guests in your party. Standard reservations accommodate between **4 and 12 people**. If your group is larger, see the note below about private events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Booking">
    Review your reservation details — location, date, time, and party size — then tap or click **Confirm Booking**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Confirmation">
    You'll receive an **email and/or SMS confirmation** immediately after booking. Keep this handy — it contains your reservation reference number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Party Sizes

| Group Size | Reservation Type |
|---|---|
| 1–3 guests | Walk-ins welcome |
| 4–12 guests | Standard reservation |
| 13+ guests | Private event booking |

<Tip>
  Hosting a larger gathering? For parties of 13 or more, our [private events page](/reservations/private-events) outlines dedicated space options, customizable packages, and full café buyout availability for up to 80 guests.
</Tip>

## Modifying or Cancelling a Reservation

Plans change — we get it. You can modify or cancel your reservation at no charge through the app, website, or by contacting the café directly.

<Note>
  Cancellations are free of charge up to **2 hours before** your reservation time. If you need to cancel within that window or simply can't make it, please call the café as soon as possible so we can open the table for other guests. We appreciate the courtesy!
</Note>

## When You Arrive

When you get to the café, head straight to the **host stand at the entrance**. Let the host know you have a reservation and share your name or confirmation number. They'll check you in and guide your group to your table right away.
