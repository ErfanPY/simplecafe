## How to Purchase

You can pick up or send a gift card through any of the following channels:

- **In-store:** Visit any Boost Café location and ask a barista at the counter to load a physical gift card with your chosen amount.
- **Online:** Head to [boostcafe.com/gift-cards](https://boostcafe.com/gift-cards), select your amount, enter the recipient's email address, and check out securely.
- **Via the app:** Open the Boost Café app, tap **Gift Cards** in the menu, choose your amount, and send it directly to any email address.

## How to Redeem

Redeeming your gift card is quick and easy, however you're ordering.

<Steps>
  <Step title="In the App">
    Open the Boost Café app and go to **Wallet → Add Gift Card**. Enter your card number or scan the barcode. The balance will be added to your app wallet and applied automatically at checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="In-Store with a Physical Card">
    At the counter, tap your physical gift card on the card reader when prompted to pay — it works just like a contactless payment. Alternatively, hand the card to your barista and they'll swipe or enter the card number for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="In-Store with a Digital Card">
    Open your gift card email or the Boost app and display the barcode. Your barista will scan it at the register, and the balance will be deducted from your card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Online or Delivery Orders">
    At checkout on the website or app, select **Gift Card** as your payment method and enter your card number. Any remaining balance after your order will stay on the card for future use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Checking Your Balance

Keeping tabs on your gift card balance is easy:

- **In the app:** Tap **Wallet** to see your current gift card balance at a glance.
- **Online:** Visit [boostcafe.com/gift-cards](https://boostcafe.com/gift-cards) and enter your card number to check your balance.
- **In-store:** Just ask any barista — they're happy to look it up for you.

</Note>

Gift cards are accepted across the full range of Boost Café ordering options:

- ✅ In-store purchases at any Boost Café location

- ✅ Delivery orders placed through the app

## Lost or Stolen Cards

<Warning>
  If your physical or digital gift card is lost or stolen, act quickly. Email us at **hello@boostcafe.com** with your **proof of purchase** (such as an order confirmation or receipt) and we'll investigate and arrange a replacement for any remaining balance. Please note that we're unable to replace balances on cards without proof of purchase, and we cannot be held responsible for unauthorized use before a loss is reported.
</Warning>
