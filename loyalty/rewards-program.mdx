Whether you stop in every morning for your usual flat white or treat yourself to a weekend pastry, Boost Café Rewards turns every purchase into something extra. Every dollar you spend earns you points that unlock free drinks, exclusive perks, and members-only events — because great coffee should always come with a little something back.

## How It Works

Earning is simple: you get **1 Boost Point for every $1 spent**, no matter how you order. Points stack up whether you're ordering at the counter, shopping through our website, or placing an order through the Boost app. Every latte, every bag of beans, every slice of banana bread counts.

Once you've accumulated **100 points**, you can redeem them for **$1 in reward credit** — applied instantly in the app or at the counter when you're ready to treat yourself.

## How to Join

Joining is completely free and takes less than two minutes. Pick the option that works best for you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Boost App or Visit Our Website">
    Head to [boostcafe.com](https://boostcafe.com) or download the Boost Café app on iOS or Android. Tap **Join Rewards** and fill in your name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Set a password, confirm your email, and your Rewards account is live. Your points balance starts at zero — but not for long.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Log in before you order (in-app, online, or by scanning your app at the counter) and every dollar you spend will automatically be added to your balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prefer to Join In-Store?">
    No problem — just ask any barista at the counter and they'll get you set up with a Rewards account on the spot. You can always link it to the app later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

    - 🥐 **Free monthly pastry** — one complimentary pastry of your choice, credited to your account on the first of each month

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="🏆 Reserve (750+ pts)">

    - 🏷️ 10% off every 10th visit

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Redeeming Your Points

When you're ready to cash in, redeeming is effortless:

- **In the app:** Go to **Rewards → Redeem Points**, choose the amount you'd like to apply, and it'll be deducted from your next order automatically.
- **At the counter:** Let your barista know you'd like to redeem points before they ring you up. They'll apply your reward credit on the spot.

The conversion rate is always **100 points = $1 reward credit**. You can redeem in any increment of 100 points, and credits can be combined with other offers unless otherwise stated.

## Points Expiry

To keep your account active, make sure you earn or redeem at least one point every **12 months**. If your account is inactive for a full year, any accumulated points will expire. You'll receive a reminder email before this happens, so you'll always have a chance to pop in for a coffee and keep your balance alive.
