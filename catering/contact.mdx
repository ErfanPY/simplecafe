## What to Expect When You Reach Out

When you get in touch, it helps to have a few basics ready: your event date, approximate headcount, event location, and any package preferences or dietary requirements. The more context you can share upfront, the faster we can get you a helpful response or a draft quote.

If you haven't already, you're also welcome to go straight to the [quote request form](/catering/request-quote) to provide your event details in one go — our coordinators check that form daily.

<Tip>
  Not sure what to ask yet? Browse our [catering packages](/catering/packages) to get a feel for what's available, or check the [FAQ](/catering/faq) for answers to our most common questions. You may find exactly what you need before needing to reach out.
</Tip>
