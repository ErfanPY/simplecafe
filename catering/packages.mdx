Boost Café offers three thoughtfully designed catering packages to suit a range of events and budgets. Whether you need a simple coffee-and-pastry setup for a morning standup or a full espresso bar with barista service for a company celebration, there's a package built for you. All packages include delivery, setup, and breakdown within the local metro area. Pricing is per person, and each package has a minimum headcount to ensure quality and freshness.

<Note>
  All prices listed are per person and do not include local delivery fees, which vary by distance and order size. Your catering coordinator will provide an itemized quote that includes all applicable fees.
</Note>

## Our Packages

<Accordion title="☀️ Morning Boost — from $8/person (min. 10 people)">
  The Morning Boost package is ideal for early meetings, breakfast events, and any occasion where you want to start the day on a high note. It keeps things simple, satisfying, and delicious.

  **Beverages:**
  - Freshly brewed drip coffee (regular & decaf)
  - Assorted hot teas
  - Orange juice

  **Food:**
  - Assorted pastry tray (croissants, muffins, and scones)

  **Service:**
  - Setup and breakdown included

  *Minimum order: 10 people | Starting at $8/person*
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="🥗 Business Lunch — from $18/person (min. 15 people)">
  The Business Lunch package is a crowd-pleasing midday spread perfect for working lunches, all-day workshops, and team events. It builds on the Morning Boost with a full lunch selection and expanded beverages.

  **Beverages:**
  - Freshly brewed drip coffee (regular & decaf)
  - Assorted hot teas
  - Orange juice
  - Iced water and lemonade

  **Food:**
  - Assorted sandwich and wrap platter
  - Seasonal grain bowls (vegetarian and protein options)
  - Fresh fruit platter
  - Snack tray

  **Service:**
  - Setup and breakdown included

  *Minimum order: 15 people | Starting at $18/person*
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="☕ Full Brew Event — from $28/person (min. 25 people)">
  The Full Brew Event package is our most immersive catering experience. A dedicated on-site barista crafts specialty espresso drinks for your guests throughout the event, alongside a full food spread and a dessert tray that finishes things off in style.

  **Beverages:**
  - Full espresso bar with on-site barista
  - All Business Lunch beverages
  - Specialty drink station featuring seasonal specials

  **Food:**
  - All Business Lunch food items
  - Dessert tray (brownies, cookies, and chocolate bark)

  **Service:**
  - On-site barista throughout the event
  - Setup and breakdown included

  *Minimum order: 25 people | Starting at $28/person*
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="✏️ Custom Package — contact us for pricing">
  Don't see exactly what you need? Our team is happy to build a custom package tailored to your group size, event type, schedule, or dietary requirements. Whether you need a vegan-only spread, a late-evening dessert reception, or something else entirely, we'll work with you to make it happen.

  **Custom packages are available for:**
  - Any group size (including groups under 10 or over 200)
  - Specific dietary requirements (vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, halal, etc.)
  - Unique event formats or hybrid food and beverage combinations
  - Multi-day or recurring office catering arrangements

  Contact our catering team to discuss your needs and receive a custom quote.
</Accordion>
