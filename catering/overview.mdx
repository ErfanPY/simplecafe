## What We Offer

Boost Café catering is designed to be flexible and stress-free. You can choose from a curated set of packages or work with our team to build something custom around your event's needs.

- **Coffee & beverage service** — drip coffee, teas, juices, and specialty espresso drinks
- **Pastry & breakfast trays** — freshly baked croissants, muffins, scones, and more
- **Lunch platters** — sandwiches, wraps, grain bowls, and seasonal salads
- **Full event catering** — end-to-end food and beverage service with an on-site barista

## Delivery & Setup

We offer delivery and full setup within the local metro area. Our catering team arrives early to have everything arranged and ready before your guests walk in, and we handle breakdown once the event wraps up. You won't need to lift a finger.

## Your Dedicated Catering Coordinator

Every booking comes with a dedicated catering coordinator who works with you from the first inquiry through the final cleanup. They'll help you select the right package, accommodate any dietary needs, and make sure every detail is dialed in before your event day.
