Have questions about how Boost Café catering works? You're in the right place. Below you'll find answers to the questions we hear most often. If something isn't covered here, don't hesitate to reach out to our catering team directly — we're always happy to help.

<Accordion title="How far in advance do I need to book catering?">
  We recommend booking at least **5–7 business days** before your event to ensure availability and give us enough time to prepare a personalized quote. For larger events (50+ people) or full espresso bar service, two weeks or more is ideal. That said, we'll always do our best to accommodate last-minute requests — reach out and we'll let you know what's possible.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is your delivery area?">
  Boost Café catering delivers throughout the **local metro area**. Delivery fees vary based on distance from our café. When you submit your quote request, include your full event address and we'll confirm availability and any applicable delivery charges in your quote. For locations outside our standard delivery zone, please contact us to discuss options.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?">
  Absolutely. We take dietary needs seriously and work hard to accommodate a wide range of requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, dairy-free, and halal options. Please list all dietary restrictions and allergies clearly in your quote request or share them with your catering coordinator as early as possible. While we do our best to prevent cross-contamination, please note that our kitchen handles common allergens.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you provide serving equipment and utensils?">
  Yes — all catering packages include the serving equipment, platters, tongs, napkins, and disposable utensils needed for your event. For espresso bar service, we bring all the equipment required to make drinks on-site. If you have specific preferences for presentation (such as reusable serveware), let your coordinator know and we'll do our best to accommodate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if my guest count changes after booking?">
  Headcount adjustments are expected and easy to handle, as long as they're communicated in time. You can update your estimated count at any point before the **72-hour final headcount deadline**. Changes submitted after that window may not be fully accommodated, and pricing may be based on the most recently confirmed count. If you expect significant fluctuations, let your coordinator know early so we can build in some flexibility.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I add a live espresso bar to any package?">
  Yes! The live espresso bar with an on-site barista is included in the **Full Brew Event** package, but it can also be added as an upgrade to the Morning Boost or Business Lunch packages for an additional per-person fee. Talk to your catering coordinator about adding espresso bar service to your booking and they'll work it into your custom quote.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I pay for catering?">
  Catering bookings require a **50% deposit** at the time of signing your catering agreement to secure your date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the event, before or upon delivery. We accept all major credit cards and ACH bank transfers. Invoices and receipts are provided electronically for easy expense tracking.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is your cancellation policy?">
  Cancellations made **5 or more business days** before the event are eligible for a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations made between **2 and 4 business days** before the event are eligible for a 50% refund of the deposit. Cancellations within **48 hours** of the event are non-refundable, as ingredients and staffing will have already been committed. If you need to reschedule rather than cancel, we'll do everything we can to find an alternative date that works.
</Accordion>
