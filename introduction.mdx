- **New to Boost Café?** Start with [Our Story](/our-story) to learn what makes us different, then check [Locations & Hours](/locations-hours) to find us.
- **Ready to order?** Head straight to [Beverages](/menu/beverages) or [Food](/menu/food) to explore the menu, then [Order Online](/ordering/online-pickup) to place your first order.
- **Planning an event?** Visit [Catering Overview](/catering/overview) to see packages and request a custom quote.
