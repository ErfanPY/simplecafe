Whether you're starting your morning with a bold double espresso or winding down with a calming chamomile, Boost Café has a drink crafted just for you. Our baristas take pride in every cup they pour — using thoughtfully sourced beans, quality teas, and wholesome ingredients to make sure every sip feels like a small moment of joy. Pull up a chair, and let us make something special.

<Tip>
  Every drink on our menu can be customized to your taste. Ask your barista about adjusting sweetness levels, milk types, or shot counts. We love making your perfect cup! **Milk alternatives** — oat, almond, soy, and coconut — are all available; a small upcharge may apply. And yes, **all espresso drinks can be made decaf** — just ask.
</Tip>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Espresso">
    Our espresso program is the heart of everything we do at Boost Café. We pull every shot fresh to order, so you get the full depth of flavor — bright, rich, and smooth.

    | Drink | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Espresso** (Single / Double) | A concentrated, velvety shot — or two — of pure coffee goodness. Bold, aromatic, and the foundation of our entire menu. |
    | **Americano** | A double shot of espresso gently diluted with hot water for a clean, full-bodied cup that lets the coffee shine. |
    | **Flat White** | Silky microfoam milk poured over a double ristretto shot. Smaller and more intense than a latte, with a creamy, smooth finish. |
    | **Cappuccino** | Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and airy foam — a classic trio that delivers balance in every sip. |
    | **Latte — Classic** | Espresso with a generous pour of steamed milk and a delicate layer of foam. Smooth, mellow, and endlessly comforting. |
    | **Latte — Vanilla** | Our classic latte sweetened with pure vanilla syrup for a warm, familiar flavor that never gets old. |
    | **Latte — Caramel** | Rich espresso and steamed milk swirled with buttery caramel syrup. Indulgent, sweet, and perfectly balanced. |
    | **Latte — Hazelnut** | A nutty, aromatic twist on the classic latte with a drizzle of hazelnut syrup that pairs beautifully with our espresso blend. |
    | **Macchiato** | Espresso "stained" with just a splash of foamy milk. Intense, bold, and elegant in its simplicity. |

    > All espresso drinks are available **decaf** upon request at no extra charge.
  </Tab>
