At Boost Café, great food is just as important as great coffee. Everything on our menu is made fresh every single day — from the golden, flaky croissants in the pastry case to the hearty grain bowls served at lunch. We believe that good ingredients, prepared with care, make all the difference. Come hungry, and leave happy.

<Info>
  We take allergies and dietary needs seriously. Items marked with **(GF)** are gluten-free. A full allergen guide — covering nuts, dairy, eggs, gluten, and more — is available at the counter or upon request. If you have a specific concern, please don't hesitate to ask a team member before ordering and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
</Info>
