Whether you're settling in with a good book, catching up with a friend, or grabbing your morning ritual on the go, Boost Café was made for you. From the very first cup we poured, our goal has been simple: bring people together over exceptional coffee in a space that feels like a second home. What started as a passion project rooted in a deep love of craft and community has grown into a neighborhood staple — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

## How It All Began

Boost Café was born out of a belief that a truly great cup of coffee is about more than just the beans. It's about the hands that source them, the care that goes into every roast, and the warmth of the space where you enjoy it. Our founders spent years exploring coffee cultures from around the world before opening our doors, drawing inspiration from the slow-poured coffees of Tokyo, the rich espresso bars of Milan, and the community-focused cafés of Melbourne. What they brought back was a simple but powerful philosophy: **do everything with intention**.

From day one, we've handcrafted every beverage to order. Our baristas don't just press buttons — they are trained artisans who understand extraction times, milk textures, and the subtle flavor profiles that make each origin unique. Whether you're sipping a silky flat white or a seasonal lavender latte, you can taste the care that went into making it.

## Handcrafted Beverages & Seasonal Ingredients

Our menu is a living, breathing thing. We work closely with local farms and regional suppliers to celebrate what's fresh and in season — meaning you'll find ingredients on our menu in October that simply won't be there in April, and that's exactly the point. From house-made syrups crafted with real fruit and botanicals to freshly baked pastries sourced from local bakeries each morning, everything on our menu is chosen because it belongs there.

You won't find artificial flavors or shortcuts in our kitchen. When you order a drink at Boost Café, you're getting the real thing — and you can taste the difference.

## Sustainable Sourcing

We take our responsibility to the planet seriously. All of our coffee beans are ethically sourced from farms that practice sustainable agriculture, pay fair wages, and invest in their communities. We partner with importers who share our values and can trace every bag of beans back to the farm where it was grown. Our cups, lids, and packaging are compostable or recyclable, and we offer a discount to every guest who brings in a reusable cup — because small habits add up.

Sustainability isn't a marketing line for us. It's woven into every decision we make, from the suppliers we choose to the energy we use to keep the lights on.

## Our Baristas

The heart of Boost Café is its people. Every one of our baristas goes through an extensive training program before they ever serve a customer. They learn the science and the art — from the chemistry of espresso extraction to the craft of free-pour latte art. But more than technique, we train our team to lead with warmth. We want you to feel genuinely welcomed every single time you walk through our doors, whether it's your first visit or your five-hundredth.

Our baristas are encouraged to get to know you, remember your order, and make your few minutes at the counter a genuine bright spot in your day.

## A Place for Everyone

No matter who you are or what brings you through our door, there's a seat for you at Boost Café. We offer a range of dairy-free milk alternatives, decaf and half-caf options, and a rotating selection of non-coffee drinks so that no one ever feels left out. Our spaces are designed to be comfortable for solo workers, group hangs, first dates, and everything in between.

We're so glad you're here — and we can't wait to make your next cup.
