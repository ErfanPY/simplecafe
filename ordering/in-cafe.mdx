Whether it's your first visit or you're a daily regular, ordering at Boost Café is designed to be a relaxed and welcoming experience. Our friendly baristas are always happy to walk you through the menu, suggest something new, or recreate your usual order exactly the way you like it. Here's what to expect when you walk through our doors.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Walk In">
    Come on in! Our café is open to everyone — whether you're grabbing a quick coffee on the way to work or settling in for a slow morning with your laptop. Take a moment to soak in the atmosphere and feel free to browse at your own pace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Menu">
    Our full menu is displayed on the boards above the counter. You'll find everything from classic espresso drinks and pour-overs to seasonal specials, teas, smoothies, and fresh pastries. Not sure what to try? Ask your barista — they love making recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Your Order">
    Walk up to the counter and let the barista know what you'd like. If it's busy, a short queue forms naturally — just follow the line and you'll be served in turn. Tell us your name so we can call it out when your order is ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Order">
    Make your drink exactly yours. You can customize:
    - **Milk type** — whole, oat, almond, soy, or coconut
    - **Temperature** — hot, iced, or blended
    - **Sweetness level** — none, light, regular, or extra sweet
    - **Extra shots** — add one or more espresso shots to any drink

    Just mention your preferences when you order and your barista will take care of the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    We accept a wide range of payment methods at the counter:
    - **Cash** — exact change appreciated but not required
    - **Credit or debit card** — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Discover
    - **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay** — tap-to-pay is fast and contactless
    - **Boost Café app** — pay directly through the app and earn loyalty points on every purchase

    Simply hand over your cash or tap/insert your card or phone at the payment terminal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Up Your Order">
    Once your drink or food is ready, your name will be called out at the pickup counter. Head over, grab your order, and you're all set. If you have any questions about your drink — or something doesn't seem right — just let us know right away and we'll fix it with a smile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dine In or Take Away — You Choose

Both are completely welcome at Boost Café. If you're staying, feel free to grab a seat at any open table. We have cozy corners, communal tables, and window spots perfect for working or catching up with a friend. If you're on the go, your takeout order comes in eco-friendly packaging that's ready to travel with you.

## Accessibility

We want Boost Café to be a comfortable place for everyone:

- Our café entrance and seating area are **wheelchair accessible**
- **High chairs** are available on request — just ask any team member
- If you need any assistance placing your order, our baristas are always happy to help

<Tip>
  Paying with the **Boost Café app** is the fastest way to check out at the counter — no fumbling for cards or cash. You'll also earn loyalty points on every purchase, which you can redeem for free drinks and food. [Download the app](#) to get started.
</Tip>
