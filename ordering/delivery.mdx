Can't make it in? No problem — Boost Café delivers. Whether you're working from home, having a lazy morning, or just need a pick-me-up without leaving the office, you can get your favorite drinks and food brought right to your door. You can order directly through the Boost Café app or use one of our third-party delivery partners: **DoorDash**, **Uber Eats**, and **Grubhub**.

<Note>
  Delivery is available within a **3–5 mile radius** of each Boost Café location. Coverage may vary by store. You can check whether delivery is available at your address when you enter your location in the app or on any third-party platform.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Boost App">
    Ordering delivery through the Boost Café app gives you the lowest delivery fee, real-time order tracking, and loyalty points on every purchase.

    **How to place a delivery order via the Boost app:**

    1. **Open the app** and sign in to your account (or create one for free).
    2. **Select "Delivery"** from the home screen, then enter or confirm your delivery address.
    3. **Browse the menu** and add items to your cart. Tap any item to customize milk type, sweetness, extras, and more.
    4. **Review your cart** — make sure your minimum order of **$10** is met before proceeding to checkout.
    5. **Choose a delivery time** — as soon as possible (estimated 20–40 minutes) or schedule for later.
    6. **Pay securely** — use a saved card, add a new card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or your Boost app balance. A flat **delivery fee of $2.99** is added at checkout.
    7. **Track your order** in real time from the **My Orders** section of the app. You'll see when your order is being prepared, when it's picked up by your delivery driver, and how far away they are.

    If something is wrong with your order or it arrives late, tap **Get Help** on your order page in the app to contact our support team instantly.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Third-Party Apps">
    You can also order Boost Café through **DoorDash**, **Uber Eats**, and **Grubhub** — great if you're already using one of these platforms or want to combine your Boost Café order with items from other restaurants.

    **How to place a delivery order via a third-party app:**

    1. **Open your preferred platform** — DoorDash, Uber Eats, or Grubhub — and make sure your delivery address is set.
    2. **Search for "Boost Café"** and select your nearest location from the results.
    3. **Browse our menu** on the platform and add your items to the cart. Customization options (milk type, sweetness, extras) are available directly in the platform interface.
    4. **Check the minimum order** — a **$10 minimum** applies on all platforms.
    5. **Checkout and pay** using whatever payment method you have saved in the third-party app. Note that **delivery fees vary by platform** and may differ from Boost app pricing — check the fee shown at checkout.
    6. **Track your order** using the tracking tools built into DoorDash, Uber Eats, or Grubhub. You'll receive status updates and a map view of your driver's location.

    For issues with third-party orders — such as a wrong or missing item, or a late delivery — please contact the support team for the platform you used. You can also reach us directly at **hello@boostcafe.com** and we'll do our best to help.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Something Wrong with Your Order?

We're sorry if your delivery didn't go as expected. Here's how to get help:

- **Via the Boost app:** Go to **My Orders**, select the affected order, and tap **Get Help** to chat with our support team.
- **Via email:** Reach us at [hello@boostcafe.com](mailto:hello@boostcafe.com) with your order number and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within one business day.
- **Via third-party apps:** For orders placed on DoorDash, Uber Eats, or Grubhub, please use the in-app support for the fastest resolution.

<Tip>
  For the best delivery experience, **order a little early** — especially during peak morning and lunch hours when demand is higher and delivery times can stretch toward the longer end. Also, take a moment to **save your delivery address** in the Boost app so you can check out in seconds next time. Your future self will thank you.
</Tip>
