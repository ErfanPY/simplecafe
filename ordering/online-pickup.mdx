---
title: "Order Ahead for Pickup at Boost Café Online"
sidebarTitle: "Online Pickup"
description: "Skip the queue by ordering ahead on the Boost Café app or website. Choose your items, pay online, and collect your order at the express counter."
---

If you'd rather skip the queue and walk straight in to a waiting order, online pickup is the way to go. Using the Boost Café app or website, you can browse the full menu, customize your drinks and food exactly how you like them, pay in advance, and choose a pickup time that works for your schedule. Your order will be ready and waiting at our express pickup counter when you arrive.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account or Continue as Guest">
    Head to the [Boost Café app](#) or [website](#) to get started. You can **create a free account** in under a minute — all you need is an email address and a password. An account lets you save your favorite orders, earn loyalty points, and store payment details for faster checkout next time. Prefer not to sign up? You're also welcome to **order as a guest** with just your name and email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Menu">
    Explore the full Boost Café menu at your leisure — no queue pressure. You'll find all our drinks, food, and seasonal specials clearly laid out by category. Tap any item to see a description, available customizations, and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Items to Your Cart">
    Tap **Add to Cart** on any item you'd like to order. You can keep adding items as you browse. Your cart is always visible at the bottom of the screen (app) or in the top navigation (website), so it's easy to review as you go.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Items">
    Before adding each item to your cart, you'll be prompted to customize it. Options include:
    - **Milk type** — whole, oat, almond, soy, or coconut
    - **Sweetness level** — none, light, regular, or extra sweet
    - **Extras** — additional espresso shots, flavor syrups, toppings, and more
    - **Size** — where applicable

    Any customizations you make online are communicated directly to our baristas, so your order arrives exactly as requested.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Pickup Location and Time">
    Select your preferred Boost Café location from the list of available stores. Then choose a **pickup time slot** — you can order as far in advance as the same day, or schedule for later in the day. We'll have your order ready right on time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Online">
    Review your cart, then head to checkout. We accept all the same payment methods as in-store, plus a few extras for your convenience:
    - **Credit or debit card** — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover
    - **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**
    - **Boost Café app balance** — top up your account balance and use it for orders
    - **Saved cards** — if you have an account, your payment details are securely stored for one-tap checkout

    Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive an order confirmation by email or push notification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrive and Collect Your Order">
    When you arrive at the café, head straight to the **express pickup counter** — no need to queue at the main counter. Your name and order number will be on your items, ready and waiting. If anything looks off or is missing, just flag down a team member and we'll sort it out immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Need to change or cancel your order? Modifications and cancellations must be made **at least 10 minutes before your scheduled pickup time**. You can manage your order in the app under **My Orders** or via the order confirmation link in your email. After the 10-minute window, orders are already being prepared and can no longer be modified.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Every online order earns you **loyalty points** on your Boost Café account — just make sure you're logged in when you check out. Points add up quickly and can be redeemed for free drinks, food, and exclusive member offers. No account yet? [Sign up for free](#) and start earning today.
</Tip>
